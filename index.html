The beginning of December 2017 I downloaded a secure messaging app called 'Signal', and was immediately messaged by a friend I haven't talked to in over a decade. He then connected me with another mutual friend who is in the information security industry as a pen tester. He and I got to chatting and I informed him of my aspirations to become the worlds most elite superhacker, and he proceeded to give me the advice he wish he had.

I strted by going to a local infosec meetup called STLSEC which turned out to be more of an industry get together and drink/tell war stories. I was able to meet a few very helpful people who made some recommendations on potential starting points for this journey. A nice man who is responsible for infosec at "Sava-a-lot" told me about this awesome program called "Launchcode" and all of the doors that it can open to me.

I went home and the next day did a bit of research on my own and really liked what I saw. On the spot I decided to take the placement/aptitude test and register to try and get accepted to the next session which was soon to start. I hit submit and wished myself luck, then in the meantime decided that I could get started on my own, and completed the Intro to Python section a day or two before receiving the notification that I was accepted to the next session!
